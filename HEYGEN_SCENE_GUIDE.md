# SoulReel — HeyGen Scene-by-Scene Build Guide

Use this while working inside HeyGen's editor. Each section = one HeyGen scene.
Copy the **Script** text directly into the scene's script box.

---

## Pre-Production Setup

**Before you start building scenes:**

1. Choose your avatar: Male, late 40s–50s, "Realistic" tier. Warm, approachable. No suit — casual button-down or henley.
2. Set default background: Living room or home office with warm lighting. Bookshelves if available.
3. Upload these assets to HeyGen's media library:
   - 3 screenshots of sample question cards from your landing page
   - 1 screenshot of the dashboard (3 content path cards visible)
   - 1 screenshot/mockup of the recording interface with AI follow-up
   - 1 screenshot of the benefactor sharing screen with access conditions
   - 1 screenshot of the hero section showing both CTA buttons (logged-out view)
   - SoulReel logo (high-res, transparent background)
   - Stock B-roll clips: family photo album, older person laughing at dinner, child looking at old photo
4. Speaking speed: Set to 0.9x (slightly slower than default)
5. Background music: Soft acoustic guitar or piano, ~15% volume

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook
**Target: 10 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
Quick question. What do you actually know about your parents' lives… before you were born?
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- Avatar: On screen, looking at camera
- Background: Living room / home office
- Overlay text: Add "What stories are you missing?" in small handwritten-style font, centered below avatar. Set to appear 3 seconds in, hold until scene ends.
- Pause: Add a 1.5-second pause after the question (use HeyGen's pause control or add "..." in the script)

---

## SCENE 2 — The Problem
**Target: 15 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
We've got home videos of birthdays and holidays. But the real stuff — why your mom chose the path she did, what your grandfather learned from his biggest failure, the quiet moments that actually shaped who they are — that just lives in their head. And one day… it won't.
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- First 5 seconds: Avatar on screen speaking
- At ~6 seconds: Switch background to B-roll montage (family photo album, older person laughing, child with old photo). Avatar voice continues as voiceover.
- Final 3 seconds ("And one day… it won't"): Return to avatar on screen
- Overlay text: Add "These stories disappear. Every day." — appears on the last line, holds 2 seconds
- Pacing: Slow down on the last line. Add a pause before "it won't."

## SCENE 3 — Introduce SoulReel
**Target: 13 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
That's why I built SoulReel. It's simple. You pick a question — something like "What's the bravest thing you've ever done?" or "Tell me about the day you became a parent." Questions designed to unlock the stories that actually matter.
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- First 3 seconds: Avatar on screen, slight smile, more energized
- At ~4 seconds: Transition to uploaded screenshots of the 3 sample question cards. Show them one at a time, ~2 seconds each:
  1. Life Story: "What's the bravest thing you've ever done?"
  2. Life Events: "Tell me about the day you became a parent."
  3. Values: "What value do you most want to pass on to the next generation?"
- Avatar voice continues as voiceover during screenshots
- Make sure the category labels ("Life Story," "Life Events," "Values & Emotions") are visible in the screenshots

## SCENE 4 — Show How Easy It Is
**Target: 14 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
You just press record and talk. No typing. No editing. Our AI listens to what you say and asks follow-up questions — like a conversation with a good friend who genuinely wants to hear more.
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- Full scene uses app screenshots/mockups as background. Avatar voice is voiceover only (or small avatar window in corner).
- Show these screens in sequence (~3 sec each):
  1. Dashboard with 3 content path cards and progress bars
  2. Recording interface — question displayed, big Record button
  3. Active conversation — user response visible, then AI follow-up question appearing (e.g., "What made that moment feel so important?")
- Overlay text callouts (floating labels):
  - Next to Record button: "No typing required"
  - Next to AI follow-up: "AI asks thoughtful follow-ups"
  - Bottom of screen: "Works on computer, phone, or tablet"

## SCENE 5 — Sharing & Legacy
**Target: 13 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
Then you choose who gets your stories. Share them with your kids now. Set a date in the future. Or create a time capsule that opens when the time is right. Your stories. Your rules. Your family gets to hear your voice — your real voice — for generations.
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- First 5 seconds: Avatar on screen, tone shifts to more meaningful/reflective
- At "time capsule": Transition to screenshot of benefactor/sharing interface showing:
  - Benefactor names (use placeholders: "Sarah," "Michael," "The Grandkids")
  - Access condition options visible: "Share immediately," "Future date," "Time capsule"
- Hold screenshot for ~3 seconds
- Return to avatar for "Your stories. Your rules."
- Overlay text: "Your stories. Your rules." — bold, centered, appears as avatar says it

  - Next to "Start Their Legacy": "Set it up for a parent or grandparent"

## SCENE 7 — Emotional Close
**Target: 10 seconds**

**Script (paste this):**
```
Because the stories that matter most… are usually the ones no one thinks to record. Until it's too late. Don't wait for someday.
```

**HeyGen setup:**
- Avatar on screen for first line. Warm family B-roll fades in softly behind.
- Deliver slowly and quietly. Not dramatic — just honest.
- After "Don't wait for someday" — 1 second pause, then:
- Fade to clean end card:
  - SoulReel logo (centered)
  - `www.soulreel.net` (below logo)
  - `Start Free Today →` (styled as a purple button)
  - Small trust row at bottom: 🔒 Encrypted · 🛡️ Your data, your control · 👁️‍🗨️ Never shared
- Hold end card for 3 seconds
- Music swells slightly during this scene, then fades out on the end card

## Post-Production Checklist

- [ ] Subtitles added (white text, dark semi-transparent background, mobile-readable)
- [ ] Background music consistent across all scenes (~15% volume)
- [ ] Transitions between scenes are simple crossfades (no flashy effects)
- [ ] All app screenshots are sharp and readable at 1080p
- [ ] End card holds for full 3 seconds
- [ ] Preview on mobile to check text readability
- [ ] Export at 1080p minimum, 16:9
- [ ] Optional: Create a 9:16 vertical cut for social/ads later
